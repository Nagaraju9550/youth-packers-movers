Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you need details, chat with our coordinator.

How is the shifting charge calculated?
Shifting charges depend on the distance between locations, the volume of cargo (determined by home size), the quality of packing required, and the labor needed for loading and unloading (like elevator availability).
Are my household goods insured during transit?
Yes! We offer transit property insurance coverage. If transit property insurance is selected, it covers accidental damages or structural impact during loading, unloading, or transit.
What packing materials do you use?
We use five-layer heavy corrugated fiber boxes, premium bubble wraps, high-grade adhesive tapes, moisture-resistant shrink films, and protective edge guards to prevent transit vibrations.
